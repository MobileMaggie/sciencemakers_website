ScienceMakers at EF Academy, Oxford

Liliana Sanchez Giraud, one of our ScienceMaker Ambassadors, has started a ScienceMakers club at EF Academy in Oxford.

For their first project, the ScienceMakers at EF are building air quality monitors using arduino boards.

They will also be making mobile data collection apps using Mozilla Appmaker, to collect data about the local environment.

They're aiming to have the apps ready to show at the AppMaker for Citizen Science session at Mozfest 2014 in London at the end of October
