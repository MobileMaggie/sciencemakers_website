ScienceMakers at Mozfest

Ravensbourne College, Saturday 25 Oct, 3-6 pm

Have a look at what we did in the workshop here!

We'll be Mozfest2014 running a workshop on building Science Apps with Appmaker, Mozilla's new tool for building mobile apps.

We're calling the workshop "Apps for Climate Change: using Appmaker for Citizen Science".

We'll be focussing on how we can use Appmaker as a citizen science tool for local data collection about the environment.

Everyone is welcome to attend, whether you're a scientist, a developer, a citizen scientist, or just curious.

We're aiming to hand the appmaker developers a wish-list of science features, so we'll need as much insight as we can get.
