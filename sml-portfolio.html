Science Museum Lates Hands-On Science Hack

Have you ever wished you could make a difference when you hear about earthquakes or flooding happening on the other side of the world?

Do you want to create a new organism by engineering DNA?

Maybe do some hardware hacking with Inupiat hunter-gatherers in the Arctic Circle?

Then YOU could become a Citizen Scientist.

Come join us at the London Science Museum's Make. Hack. Do. for an evening of hands-on citizen science!

Check out our video of the event on the Citizens of Science Youtube Channel.
